Our History

Owners and Pharmacists Steven and Karen purchased the business in 1993. Formerly known as Smith's Pharmacy, this shop has been serving the community since 1928.

30 years later, the team has grown to provide authentic and friendly care to the community.

Book a Vaccination

Steve's Pharmacy offers free routine vaccination services that you can book online.

You can obtain your immunisation history by visiting the Australian Immunisation Register (AIR).
Vaccinations are conducted by a medical professional in-store and will take approximately 15 minutes.

To make a booking, please click the button below and select an available timeslot. You will be required to provide some contact information.

Please arrive on time! If you are more than five (5) minutes late, your appointment may be cancelled to ensure the next person has sufficient time.
